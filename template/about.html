Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Luxora

Our products are carefully curated from premium manufacturers and artisans worldwide. Each item undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure it meets our exacting standards for craftsmanship, materials, and design. We focus on timeless pieces that combine functionality with aesthetic appeal.

We work directly with authorized dealers and manufacturers to source all our products. Each item comes with proper documentation and certificates of authenticity where applicable. Our team verifies every product before it reaches our warehouse, and we offer a 100% authenticity guarantee.

We offer a 30-day return policy for all items in their original condition. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and accompanied by proof of purchase. Return shipping is free for defective items, and we provide prepaid return labels for your convenience. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can track your package using the provided tracking number on our website or the carrier's website. We also send updates via email and SMS as your package moves through the delivery process.
